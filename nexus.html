About The Nexus

The Nexus is a workspace that serves as a supportive background for young people who aspire to learn coding and digital skills. We pair training with real employment opportunities and community-driven projects.

Departments

  • Administrative Department (entrance & centre table with CAO)
  • Purchase Department (funding & programs)
  • Conference Room ("Extension")

Workspace culture

We use technology responsibly and celebrate African culture — displaying leaders and stories that inspire our community.

Our promise

Comfortable seats, reliable sockets, clean facilities and safe spaces for learning and collaboration.

Coding Class & Courses

We teach a range of subjects suitable for different age groups and skill levels:

  • Introduction to computers & digital literacy
  • Web development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Graphic design & visual animation
  • Python programming & data basics
  • Java fundamentals
  • Intro to cybersecurity

Classes are project-based and culminate in a showcase where students present a final project.

Employment & Opportunities

We help learners transition into work through:

  • Placement with partner companies
  • On-site freelancing and contract work
  • Resume & interview preparation
  • Job board and application support

If you have a job listing or would like to partner, please contact us.

Restaurant

Good food fuels great work. Our on-site restaurant serves affordable meals and refreshments — local favourites like pounded yam, spaghetti, rice dishes and snacks.

Standards & hygiene are strictly maintained. Ask about daily combos and discounts for students.
